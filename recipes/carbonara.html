Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish that combines creamy egg-based sauce with crispy pancetta and parmesan cheese. This comforting meal is quick to prepare and full of rich flavors, perfect for any pasta lover.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil and cook the diced pancetta until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the spaghetti, reserving 1/2 cup of the cooking water.
  5. Toss the hot spaghetti with the pancetta mixture. Remove from heat.
  6. Quickly stir in the egg mixture, using the residual heat to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved water as needed to adjust the consistency.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
