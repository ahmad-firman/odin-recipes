Fried Rice Recipe

Fried rice is a beloved dish enjoyed worldwide for its simplicity, versatility, and satisfying flavors. Originally from Asia, this meal is a perfect way to repurpose leftover rice and transform it into a mouthwatering dish. The combination of fluffy rice, savory seasonings, and an assortment of vegetables or proteins creates a wholesome and customizable meal that can be enjoyed any time of day.

Ingridients

  • 2 cups of cooked and cooled rice
  • 2 tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and chopped onion to the pan, cooking until fragrant and translucent.
  4. Push the ingredients to one side of the pan, pour in the beaten eggs, and scramble until just cooked.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the pan, stirring to combine with the other ingredients.
  6. Pour in the soy sauce. Mix thoroughly, ensuring the rice is evenly coated.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve warm.
